by (Ahmed Mohamed Oweda)¶

Investigation Overview¶

This exploration and visualization study mainly focuses on finding the bike usage and users categories and the avaerage duration for trips in the 12-month ride trips data in 2018 from January to December.We want to generate relationships between the trips duaration and the distance covered and the user types and the time intervals of trips .

Dataset Overview¶

Bay Wheels (previously known as Ford GoBike) is a public bike sharing system in the San Francisco Bay Area in California.The dataset used for this exploratory analysis consists of files containing monthly individual trip data from January 2018 to December 2018 in CSV format.These files were downloaded and gathered together to form one bog dataset containing some information like type of users , start and end time , start and end points and duration of the trips .

After Gathering all the data we made the following tasks :

  • Assess the data visually and programmatically
  • Determine Quality issues and Tidiness issues
  • Clean the data
  • Add new columns to the data by making some calculations as distance , starting day , starting month and starting hour
  • Save the new-cleaned data to a csv file

Summary¶

  • Subscriber users are much more than Customer users .
  • Bikes shared for trips represent 91.3% in the total trips of the year 2018.
  • Subscribers use bikes in weekdays more while customers use bikes in weekends more.
  • The average trip duration all over the year is 500 seconds ( around 8.5 minutes ).
  • The highest count of trips occur in October month , and the lowest in January (may be due to heavy ice falling) .
